SMB:// Syntax and Usage

smb://[[[ntdomain;]user[:password]@]server[:port]][/share[/path][/file]]

  • smb:// brings up a list of available Workgroups.
  • The server field can be either a Workgroup or a Fileserver.
  • Fileservers may be identified by NetBIOS Names, DNS names, or IP.
