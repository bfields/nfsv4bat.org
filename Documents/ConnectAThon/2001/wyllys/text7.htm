KDC Database Compatibility

  • Importing and exporting the principal database

  • kdb5_util - utility for dumping and loading db

    • 4 DB formats supported:
      • Version 4 (beta 7 and later) - DEFAULT
      • Version 3 (beta 6)
      • Version 2 (beta 5 and earlier)
      • Ovsec-adm-export - OpenVision format

  • SEAM and MIT both support all 4 formats

  • Microsoft does not support any of these.
