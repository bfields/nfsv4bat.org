Summary

  • A working test suite has been developed (although not complete)

  • The test suite covers approximately 70 % of the NFSv4 operations

    • State-related operations like OPEN are not tested

  • pynfs includes additional tools, which can be used in other projects

  • pynfs is OpenSource. More information: http://www.cendio.se/~peter/pynfs/
